Chimmy

Founded in the Philippines, Chimmy is a Yogurt drink shop which offers a variety of flavors blended to perfection enhanced to suit the local taste buds. Chimmy is now setting foot in different parts of the Philippines to let everyone #ExperienceEuphoria in every sip. As a brand that delivers #OriginalFilipinoblendYogurt drinks, Chimmy lets everyone enjoy the health benefits of yogurt without it being too sour and just the right amount of sweetness perfect for the Filipino palate. Chimmy offers the best of both worlds as it satisfies your cravings to indulge and at the same time provide you with a healthy body.
